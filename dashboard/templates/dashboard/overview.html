Core research question

Did Twitter/X network visibility reflect actual Senate election outcomes, or did it mainly reveal online attention, controversy, amplification, and community structure?

Main finding: online and network metrics aligned strongly with votes, but did not perfectly reproduce the Top 12 winners.

Network science logic

  • Nodes: candidates, hashtags, users, topics
  • Edges: co-mentions, co-occurrences, replies, candidate-hashtag links
  • Metrics: PageRank, weighted degree, betweenness, communities, density
  • Outcome layer: actual vote rank and Top 12 winner labels
