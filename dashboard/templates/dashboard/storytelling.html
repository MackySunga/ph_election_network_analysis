Temporal Storytelling

How the election discourse unfolded over time

The animated outputs explain how the data was conceived as a time-dependent communication process. Instead of showing only final rankings, this page shows when conversation volume rose, how candidates accumulated attention, and how hashtags became connective discourse markers.

Why animation matters here

The final network graphs show structure, but the animations show formation. They help users see how online attention emerged day by day before it became a final network metric.

  • Tweet volume shows when the conversation became active.
  • Candidate races show how visibility accumulated.
  • Hashtag growth shows how discourse labels became hubs.
  • Daily pulses show short-term spikes and moments of salience.

Story logic

Time → Attention → Hashtags → Networks → Outcome Comparison

The dashboard now supports the full narrative: online discourse did not appear all at once. It evolved through time, producing the final network centrality and alignment results.
